Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw

Ginataang kalabasa at sitaw is a traditional filipino dish made with squash and string beans cooked in coconut milk. it is simple, healthy, and best served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs squash (kalabasa)
  • 1 bunch string beans (sitaw)
  • 3 ounces pork belly
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 1 onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • ginger
  • cooking oil
  • fish sauce and pepper

Method

  1. heat oil and cook pork until brown
  2. add garlic, onion, and ginger
  3. pour coconut milk and boil
  4. let it simmer
  5. add squash
  6. add string beans
  7. season to taste
  8. serve hot
