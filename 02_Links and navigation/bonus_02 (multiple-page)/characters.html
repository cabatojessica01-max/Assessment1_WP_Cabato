Characters

Dr. Temperance Brennan (nicknamed “Bones”) is the main forensic anthropologist in the series. She is extremely intelligent, highly logical, and relies heavily on science and evidence when solving cases. Over time, she slowly develops stronger emotional awareness through her relationships and experiences with the team.

Special Agent Seeley Booth is an FBI agent and former Army sniper who works closely with Brennan. He is instinctive, emotional, and often acts as the moral compass of the team. His partnership with Brennan is the heart of the series, balancing science with human intuition.

Angela Montenegro is the forensic artist of the Jeffersonian team. She uses advanced computer graphics and artistic reconstruction to help visualize victims and crime scenes. She also provides emotional support and helps maintain balance within the group.

Dr. Jack Hodgins is an entomologist and mineralogist who specializes in analyzing insects, soil, and trace evidence. His scientific discoveries often provide key breakthroughs in solving difficult cases, making him an essential part of the investigative team.

Dr. Camille Saroyan (Cam) is the head of the forensic division at the Jeffersonian. She oversees autopsies and manages the lab team. Cam is highly professional, strong-willed, and ensures that investigations run smoothly while maintaining order within the team.
