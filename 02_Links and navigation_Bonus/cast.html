Cast

Bones features a strong ensemble cast that remained central throughout its long run. The actors portrayed forensic experts, FBI agents, and supporting characters who became essential to the development of the series.

  • Emily Deschanel as Dr. Temperance Brennan
    Lead character and forensic anthropologist known for her intelligence, scientific precision, and logical thinking.

  • David Boreanaz as Seeley Booth
    FBI Special Agent and Brennan’s long-time partner who brings emotional intelligence and field experience.

  • Michaela Conlin as Angela Montenegro
    Forensic artist responsible for reconstructing faces and crime scenes using advanced technology and creativity.

  • T.J. Thyne as Dr. Jack Hodgins
    Specialist in entomology and trace evidence, known for uncovering hidden scientific clues.

  • Tamara Taylor as Dr. Camille Saroyan
    Head of the forensic division who manages the Jeffersonian team and oversees all lab investigations.

  • John Francis Daley as Dr. Lance Sweets
    FBI psychologist who helps analyze behavior and psychological motives behind criminal cases.

  • Eric Millegan as Dr. Zack Addy
    A brilliant young intern who works closely with Brennan in earlier seasons, known for his intelligence and analytical skills.
